Where it all began

One of the first Japanese objects I bought is this stoneware ‘Nippon Shoyu’ Japanese soy sauce bottle. It is dated in the first half of the 20th century and probably travelled as a souvenir to Western Europe. On top it states 標高, which loosely translates to ‘elevated, height above sea level’. In the middle part the brand name 富士 is written: ‘Fuji’, like the mountain ;) Below its says 日本 (nihon) and 醤油 (shouyu), ’nippon shoyu’. Inspired by this bottle, I created my own Fuji logo and called this feed nipponshoyu.
